Movies Summary Feedback

Aparichit

Ramanujam, who suffers from multiple personality disorder, works as a lawyer by day and a vigilante at night. He uses tips from the 'Garuda Purana' as his tools to expose various antisocial elements.

Shankar I

Soulful and authentic, this masterfully crafted action-oriented romantic thriller follows the twists and turns in the life of Lingesan, a bodybuilder whose aim is to become the champion of the Mr Tamil Nadu contest.

Ratsasan

Following his father's death, a man gives up on his dream to become a film-maker and joins the police force. He then attempts to track down a psychopathic killer who targets schoolgirls.

Darna Mana Hai

Six friends relate spooky tales to one another while stranded on a highway. Each story turns out to be scarier than the next one but the biggest horror strikes when some of the friends go missing.

Darna Zarori Hai

Six children lose their way in a forest in the middle of the night. Later, they reach a haunted house where an old woman challenges them to sit through a narration of six horrific stories.
